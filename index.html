What type of loan products do we offer?

At Flat Fee Biz Loans we offer asset-based Investment Residential, Multi-family / Mixed-Use and Commercial Mortgage Loan Products. We have short-term and long term options for every scenario. We also finance residential REHAB projects with easy-to-understand qualification guidelines.

How long does it take us to close?

Although processing delays can happen with any loan, we strive to close all of our loans within 7-21 days from submission. Even more - We strive to keep our borrowers informed every step of the way. If there is a delay - You will know why, and we’ll never leave you in the dark.

$1495. That’s it. Any loan, any size. That’s all we charge. As is with any loan - you’ll have third party fees, but none of them will be from us. No B.S. processing fees, no back-end fees on the HUD - nothing. And most importantly - NO BROKER POINTS. Just a $1495 processing fee, and that’s it.
